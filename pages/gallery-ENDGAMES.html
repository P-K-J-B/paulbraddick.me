ENDGAMES: MATERIAL IMAGINARY

My exhibition for Leeds Beckett University's Masters of Art & Design end-of-year show, class of 2014. Taking inspiration from John Carpenter's 1988 film "They Live", I worked from a single photograph taken of an empty space and created a loosely rhythmic sequence of images that depict a mixture of innate and cultural compulsions; placing them on a mirror in front of an illusory "endless corridor".
